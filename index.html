How the Savings Tracker Works

We calculate your savings based on either a percentage of your income or the amount left after expenses. You can also add an interest rate to see how much your savings could grow.

Formula Used:

  • If savings % is entered:
    Monthly Savings = Income × (Savings % ÷ 100)
  • Otherwise:
    Monthly Savings = Income - Expenses
  • Total Savings after months:
    Total = Monthly Savings × Months
  • If Interest Rate is given:
    Total Savings = Total × (1 + Interest Rate ÷ 100)
